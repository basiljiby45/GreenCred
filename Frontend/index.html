Welcome to GreenCred

Turn Your Recycling into Rewards!

GreenCred is a web-based platform that encourages users to recycle waste by awarding points for every submission. Track your contributions and redeem earned points for eco-friendly products or coupons from our rewards shop. Our system motivates eco-friendly habits through gamification and community leaderboards, making recycling rewarding, engaging, and accessible to everyone.

About GreenCred

Our Mission

Launched in 2025, GreenCred combats environmental pollution by turning waste into rewards, empowering a sustainable future.

How It Works

  • Sign Up: Join as a customer or volunteer.
  • Submit Waste: Earn points for every kilogram recycled.
  • Track Progress: Monitor contributions on your dashboard.
  • Redeem Rewards: Shop for eco-friendly items like tote bags and water bottles.

Gamification & Community

Compete on leaderboards, earn badges, and support community projects like tree planting with a portion of your points.

Our Vision

By 2030, GreenCred aims to make recycling a global habit, fostering a greener planet through accessible, rewarding participation.
